The Mountain
Total course area 130ha
Altitude difference 540m (minimum elevation 460m, maximum elevation 1,000m)
Maximum run distance 3,500m (Subaru → Procion → Volks → Vega)
Slope total run distance 11.6km
Maximum slope 45 degrees (Orion)
Average slope 17 degrees

* We are not responsible for any accidents or injuries in this slope.

* The course may be closed due to course conditions and special events. Please note.

* Depending on the course, it may not be possible to maintain a snowstorm due to snow conditions.

* Drone flying over the premises (piste, facility, parking lot) is prohibited at Kamidate Kogen Ski Area.

Facilities
Resort Hall

The resort hall, located in the base parking lot, has major facilities such as Kaminoyu, salon, event hall, shops and rentals.
Locker Room

Place your luggage in the locker room and change to snowwear before going to the slopes!
Women's Locker

All women's locker rooms have Dyson dryers! Please enjoy the professional finish used in the beauty salon.
Lala House

“LaLa HOUSE” on the first floor of the resort hall is a stylish pub with a bar counter. There is also a set menu in the daytime, and at night it turns into a pub-style bar that handles many types of liquor.
Kebab No Mori

If you feel hungry, go to the kitchen car. In front of the resort hall, “Kebab no Mori” offers hot kebab-centered hot snacks.
Guerespo

We have items essential for enjoying the slopes, such as helmets and goggles.
Souvenir Shop

There are plenty of souvenirs from Niigata and Echigo Yuzawa! Please drop in before you return.
4-6F Kaminoyu

Opening hours: Weekdays 12:00 to 18:00 (Bathing only); Friday 18:00 to Sunday 18:00 (Last check-in 17:30)
